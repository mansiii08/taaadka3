Differentiating Between Cold and Flu

Recognizing the symptoms of the common cold and the flu is vital. While both illnesses share some similarities, the flu tends to be more severe and may require medical attention. Understanding the differences can help you seek the right treatment.

Staying Healthy Through Prevention

Preventing the spread of cold and flu is crucial. Practicing good hygiene, such as frequent handwashing and covering your mouth when coughing or sneezing, can help reduce the risk of infection. Additionally, maintaining a healthy lifestyle can boost your immune system.
